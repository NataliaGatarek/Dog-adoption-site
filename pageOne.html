Before adoption

So you decided to adop a dog, that is briliant. But before it, you should research the breeds to be fully aware what is the heritage of your dog.Every singe dog is a mix of two or more breed of dogs.Scientists used genetic testing in over 900 shelter dogs to identify breed heritage in the largest study of its kind. The researchers found widespread genetic diversity: 125 breeds in the sample and an average of three breed matches per dog. The accuracy of shelter staff in identifying more than one breed in the dog's heritage based just on physical appearance was only 10 percent. How breed labels can impact shelter dogs is discussed.

Sporting Dogs

Sporting dogs are used as aids in human sports such as game shooting, where they are used to find, chase and retrieve game birds.

Show more...Typically small-medium, they are highly obedient and trainable, energetic and naturally alert, as well as excellent swimmers. They have good stamina that allows them to remain active throughout a long hunt, and as such require a lot of exercise even if kept as a pet. An example of a sporting dog breed is the American Water Spaniel, often used as a gun dog.
